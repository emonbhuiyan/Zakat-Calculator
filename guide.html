Zakat Guide

Nisab (Minimum Wealth Required for Zakat)

The Nisab is the minimum amount of wealth a Muslim must have before they are liable to pay Zakat.

  • Gold: 87.48 grams (24K)
  • Silver: 612.36 grams
  • Cash: Equivalent to 87.48g of gold

Who is Eligible to Receive Zakat?

  • The poor and needy
  • Those in debt
  • New Muslims
  • Travelers in need
  • Slaves seeking freedom
  • Those striving in the cause of Allah
